It's important to understand the effects that CBD has on specific conditions, however, we must broaden the scope to understand the correlation between CBD, the human body, and the interaction between the two. Then we can begin to understand how this natural compound may have therapeutic benefits.

First, we must acknowledge the human body and its endocannabinoid system (ECS). The ECS is important in the function of the brain, endocrine, nervous, and immune systems. It has strong evidence that it plays an important regulatory role of hormones relating to reproductive functions and response to stress. The ECS system manages our health by creating balance within the body.

When the endocannabinoid system becomes unbalanced, the systems in which the ECS controls will begin to malfunction, which can lead to decreased immune function and diminished wound healing. The body seeks to regulate itself again and find that healthy equilibrium which CBD may aid in.
